About the Work

This is a work-from-home data entry project. We provide you with important documents to process. Because of strict project timelines, failing to complete or abandoning work midway results in a loss for the company. Therefore, an initial payment is required to ensure accountability and project commitment.

Payment Cycles

There is no fixed salary date. You get paid as soon as you submit your files. Once your work is cleared, you are free to purchase new projects based on your requirements.

Important Notice

You will be handling sensitive documents such as Aadhaar cards, Passports, PAN cards, and Form-16s. Do not attempt to sell or misuse this data. If we find any misuse, strict legal action will be taken.

Rules & Procedure

  • You must submit your Aadhaar card for verification.
  • Once you purchase a job, you will receive the entry portal/details within 2 hours.
  • The work involves simple Excel data entry.
  • We will provide full guidance on exactly what entries need to be made for each document.
